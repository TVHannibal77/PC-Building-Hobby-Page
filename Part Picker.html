Where to Buy PC Parts



Newegg

Excellent place to buy PC parts but as of recent years. They took on the trend of allowing 3rd party retailers so beware.

Check it Out
Microcenter

This is my favorite store. One of the only places to look at PC parts in person. Excellent store for all PC needs that you dont want to wait for.

Check it Out
Amazon

I typically don't buy PC parts from Amazon if I don't have too due to handeling and 3rd party retailers. If you absolutly have to then it is alright but watch out for broken parts.

Check it Out
Bestbuy

Doesn't have the biggest selection of hardware but good for prepherials. Note: You can price match to Amazon aslong as its sold and shipped from Amazon.
