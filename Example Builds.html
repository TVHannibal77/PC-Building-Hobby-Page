Value of Custom Builds

#1. Overall Cost

Typically you can build a entire PC with the same specifications as the prebuilt for less.


#2. Customization

When building a custom PC you can use whatever case, lights etc that you want allowing you to personalize the build.


3. Quality Control

The main issue with prebuilts is that they skimp out on certain parts such as RAM, hard drives, fans. Items that a consumer that is only somewhat knowledgeable wouldnt notice.
