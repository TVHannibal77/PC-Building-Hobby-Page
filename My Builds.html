My First Build

Although I don't have a picture of my first build it was a hand me down from my dad. At this time I knew nothing about building PCs. This PC was a older dual core with a very early graphics card and like maybe 4gb of RAM. Funny store is one day I decided I was going to clean my computer not knowing any better I took it all apart and saw there was some gunky stuff on the processer so I cleand it off aswell as wiping down everything else. Not knowing that was about to fry my computer I plugged it all in and hit that power button needless to say that computer didn't turn on anymore after that.
