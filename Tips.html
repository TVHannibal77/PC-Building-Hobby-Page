Thermal Paste and Front Panel Connecters



There may be more methods but these are the most common. Personally I use the X method and have had good results. As you can see the diffrence between each method a very slim margin.

Thermal Paste


Front Panel connecters can be the easiest or most difficult task when building a new PC. If your one of the lucky ones check your motherboard box to see if they gave you a connecter adapter. This little block makes life easy allowing you to just plug the cables into in then slide it on the motherboard.
