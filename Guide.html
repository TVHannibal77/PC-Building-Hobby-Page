Quick Guide to Building a PC



Put on anti-static bracelet and attach it to the metal part of your PC case. Unbox all parts and prepare the case. At this time you can also install hard drives and the power supply.

Attach hardware to the motherboard such as the RAM. Socket and apply themal paste to the processer. Make sure the cooler is tight but not too tight. Depending on graphics card size wait to socket it till the motherboard is in the case.

Screw motherboard into case. Socket graphics card if you havn't done so already. Work on front panel connecters. Then work on attaching the rest of the cables from the power supply to the motherboard and other hardware. Ensure to plug in hard drive sata cables aswell.

Work on cable management people have a tendency to skip this step and forget to do it later on.
